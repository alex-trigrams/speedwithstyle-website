The difference

We teach proper swimming from lesson one.

Plenty of swim schools get kids across the pool and call it a day. We start with technique: body position, breathing, a stroke that holds up when kids get tired.

So most kids who start with us at 5 or 6 are swimming 1 to 2km by the time they're 8 or 9.

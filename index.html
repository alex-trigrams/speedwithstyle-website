The difference

We teach proper swimming from lesson one.

Plenty of swim schools get kids across the pool and call it a day. We start with technique: body position, breathing, a stroke that holds up when kids get tired.

So most kids who start with us at 5 or 6 are swimming 1 to 2km by the time they're 8 or 9.

Why choose us

Five reasons parents pick SWS

Max 4, guaranteed

Never more than four kids to a class. Any more than that and it stops being coaching.

Warm, heated water

12.5m at 33°C and 25m at 28°C. Warm water means no blue lips and more time in the pool learning.

The best instructors in WA

Every coach is a current or former squad swimmer who knows real technique.

No rigid levels

Kids move up when they're ready, based on how they swim. No badge chart to get stuck on.

Fun, fast progress

Engaged kids learn faster. Have fun, learn fast, swim fast: that's the whole idea.
